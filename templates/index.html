The goal of the Lithuanian Catholic Academy of Sciences (LCAS):

To create a community of Lithuanian scholars of Catholic outlook with diverse academic backgrounds:
- by keeping Christian thought in the foreground
- by advancing the achievements and projects of its members
- by helping members network and share ideas
